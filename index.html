About Pravan Coirs

Based in the heart of coconut agriculture in Pollachi, Tamil Nadu, Pravan Coirs is a specialized manufacturer of premium coco peat blocks. We are dedicated to transforming nature's bounty into sustainable solutions for horticulture and agriculture worldwide.

With years of experience in the coir industry, we have built a reputation for excellence in producing high-quality coir pith blocks. We oversee every step of the manufacturing process to ensure our blocks meet strict quality, expansion, and sustainability standards.

Our Premium Coir Blocks

We specialize in high-quality compressed coco peat blocks for agricultural, horticultural, and domestic applications.

5KG Coir Pith Blocks

Premium 5KG compressed coco peat blocks with high expansion ratio and excellent water retention. Ideal for commercial nurseries and potting soil mixtures.

650g Coir Briquettes

Compact, easy-to-handle 650g coco peat briquettes. Perfect for home gardening, retail potting mixes, and small-scale horticultural use.

Coco Grow Bags

Ready-to-use coir block slabs in UV-treated grow bags. Specifically formulated for hydroponic cultivation of tomatoes, cucumbers, and berries.

Our Manufacturing Process

From sourcing nature's finest coconuts to delivering premium coir blocks, we ensure quality at every step.

1. Sourcing

We source premium coconut husks directly from local farms in Pollachi.

2. Washing & Buffering

The coir pith is aged, washed thoroughly to lower EC levels, and dried under sunlight.

3. Compressing

Pith is sifted, weighed, and subjected to high-pressure compression into solid blocks.

4. Global Delivery

Blocks are safely palletized, strapped, and shipped into containers for global delivery.

Frequently Asked Questions

Find answers to common questions about our coir products

What is the expansion ratio of your 5KG coir blocks?

Our premium 5KG coir blocks have an excellent expansion ratio. A single 5KG block can expand to yield approximately 70 to 75 liters of moist coco peat when hydrated.

Are your coir blocks washed (Low EC)?

Yes, we offer both washed (Low EC < 0.5 mS/cm) and unwashed (High EC) coir blocks. For most agricultural and horticultural needs, our washed low EC blocks are highly recommended.

Do you offer international shipping and bulk orders?

Absolutely. As a direct manufacturer, we regularly ship container loads globally. We can accommodate large bulk orders with customized packaging requirements.
